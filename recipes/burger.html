Burger

Description

An american dish made up of bread, lettuce, tomatos and chicken or beef with some sauce of choice

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomato slices
  • Meat of choice
  • Lettuce
  • Sauce of choice

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Toast the bread
  3. Cut lettuce and tomatos
  4. Arrange Ingredients and put sauce on top
  5. Enjoy!
