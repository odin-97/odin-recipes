Pizza

Description

An italian dish made of bread, tomato sauce, cheese and additional toppings.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Additional Toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Fluff and flatten bread
  2. Add tomato sauce and cheese
  3. Put additional toppings on top
  4. Slide in oven and heat for 12 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
